Nirajan Kadel

Nirajan Kadel is a young, up rising Nepalese singer from Kathmandu. Born in 1994, to father Radheshyam Kadel and mother Janaki Devi Kadel, he grew up around music and melody. In fact, his father was his inspiration who himself sang classical and folk songs and played several instruments such as flute, guitar, harmonium, tabla. At a very young age , he started to sing along with his father and won people's heart with his voice. His musical career started in 2013 with his first debut album PARIKSHYA with his unmissable hit “Dil Maya” which has been appreciated by the music community who discover the voice and the charm of Nirajan Kadel. His talent has been awarded by being nominated for "Best Debut Artist" in 2013 at Kalika FM Music Award for his song "Dil Maya". Although, Nirajan doesn't have a formal training, he is dedicated, devoted and a self taught singer who has won the hearts of hundreds of the listeners. Until now, he has recorded a dozen of songs and five music videos. Recently, his duet song with melodious singer Prabisha Adhikari has been nominated for "Best Duet Song" in the Himalayan International Music Award 2020. Nirajan Kadel is a humble, genuine, fun and charismatic personality. He has devoted himself to follow the path of music and entertain millions of people along with his musical journey as he defines "Music is the feelings which is felt every moment". So ladies and gentlemen let's seat back - relax - and get ready to be carried away in this journey with him!
